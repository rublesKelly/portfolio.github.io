    About Me

    Experienced and accomplished data analyst, UI designer and software developer looking to focus my career on data analytics and database management. I have worked on large international design projects within and across multi disciplinary teams using the agile project management approach.

    As a design consultant, I honed my ability to effectively convey ideas and information visually to various audiences. My time at the Central Bank of Ireland and SAP further sharpened these skills, allowing me to build consensus and maintain positive relationships with clients and cross-functional teams. I am currently employed with the central bank of Ireland as a software developer specialising in SharePoint and PowerBI.
